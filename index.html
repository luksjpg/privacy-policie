Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. To provide a connected experience where you can access your garden on multiple devices (phone or computer) via login, the following data may be processed:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address).
  • Account credentials (email address) used for logging in.
  • Game data, including plot management, seed planting, and harvest progress[cite: 1].
  • Operating system and time spent on the Application.

The Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device. It does not use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to process your data.
